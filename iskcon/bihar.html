Radha Banke Bihari Ji Mandir - Buddha Marg, Patna

Sri Sri Radha Banke Bihari Ji Mandir, located on Buddha Marg in Patna, is a grand ISKCON temple dedicated to Lord Krishna and Radha. Known for its beautiful architecture and vibrant spiritual atmosphere, it serves as a center for devotion, meditation, and cultural events in Bihar.
