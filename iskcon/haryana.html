Sri Krishna Arjuna Mandir- Kurukshetra

Sri Krishna Arjuna Mandir, located in Jyotisar, Kurukshetra, Haryana, is an ISKCON temple commemorating the sacred site where Lord Krishna delivered the Bhagavad Gita to Arjuna. Developed in a grand chariot-shaped design, the temple hosts daily worship, kirtans, and spiritual teachings, and is a prominent center for devotees and pilgrims.

Temple

History

Sri Krishna–Arjuna Mandir in Jyotisar, Kurukshetra is an ISKCON temple built at the sacred site where Lord Krishna is believed to have spoken the Bhagavad Gita to Arjuna. Inspired by Srila Prabhupada’s 1975 visit, ISKCON acquired the land in 1996, and the temple’s foundation was laid in 2015. The temple is designed in the shape of a grand chariot and is becoming a major spiritual center.
