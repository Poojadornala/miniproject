Radha Krishna Chandra Mandir- Rajajinagar,Bengaluru

Sri Sri Radha Krishna Chandra Mandir in Rajajinagar, Bangalore, is a large ISKCON temple established in 1997. It offers a serene spiritual environment and hosts vibrant festivals and community programs.

Temple

History

Established through the donation of the original Jagannath deities and property to ISKCON in 1979, the temple became a spiritual center dedicated to Lord Jagannath, Baladev, and Subhadra. It has since grown through regular devotional services, youth outreach, and significant events—including the first ISKCON program in Ranchi held in February 2025—solidifying its role in the region's Krishna-consciousness movement .
