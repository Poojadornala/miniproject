Radha Parthasarathi Mandir- East of Kailash,New Delhi

Shri Radha Krishna Mandir established on January 24, 2016 with Shri Ganesh and Shri Hanumant Lal by temple committee in Dwarka sub-city of Delhi. During famous festival Shivaratri, 52 pairs are performed Maha Rudrabhishek on Shivling.

Temple

Festivals

Shivaratri, Janmashtami, Shri Krishn Chhati, Navratri, Diwali, Hanuman Jayanti|Hanuman Janmotsav, Ganeshotsav|Ganesha Chaturthi, Guru Purnima, International Yoga Day, Sharad Purnima, Karwa Chauth, Chhath Puja, Govardhan Puja, Gopashtami, Tulsi Vivah, Sthapana Diwas, Vasant Panchami, Holi

Activities

On every Tuesday and Saturday, the temple organized musical Sunderkand Path at 5 PM. After Sunderkand Path, few information related to Hindu religion, rites and rituals is explained and exchanged. Regular bhandaras are done on the Hanuman Janmotsav, Navratri and on the establishment day in the temple. In the temple premises, the green natural environment is also taken care along with the provision of cold water for drinking. On the occasion of Shri Hanuman Janmotsav, Havan, Sunderkand and Bhandara are organized in the temple.

Bhagavad Gita Slokam

Chapter 9, Verse 18

गतिर्भर्ता प्रभु: साक्षी निवास: शरणं सुहृत् |
प्रभव: प्रलय: स्थानं निधानं बीजमव्ययम् || 18||

gatir bhartā prabhuḥ sākṣhī nivāsaḥ śharaṇaṁ suhṛit
prabhavaḥ pralayaḥ sthānaṁ nidhānaṁ bījam avyayam

Translation

I am the Supreme Goal of all living beings, and I am also their Sustainer, Master, Witness, Abode, Shelter, and Friend. I am the Origin, End, and Resting Place of creation; I am the Repository and Eternal Seed.
