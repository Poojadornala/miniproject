Radha Gopinath Dasavatara Mandir -Rajahmundry

Sri Sri Radha Govinda Mandir in Tirupati, built with lotus-inspired architecture, houses beautiful deities including Giriraj Govardhan and Ashta Sakhi. The temple features a Goshala, a spiritual academy, and a Govinda restaurant serving prasadam. It regularly hosts cultural programs and spiritual workshops, fostering devotion and community learning.

History

Sri Sri Radha Gopinath Dasavatara Mandir in Rajahmundry was inaugurated in August 2006 on a sacred 2-acre site along the banks of the Godavari River. The temple is uniquely designed with ten mantaps, each representing one of the Dasavatara (ten incarnations) of Lord Vishnu. It also features a Vedic exhibition and regularly hosts spiritual events like Govinda Srinivas Abhishekam and traditional musical offerings. The temple stands as a vibrant center for devotion, education, and cultural heritage.

Bhagavad Gita Slokam

Chapter 9, Verse 24

अहं हि सर्वयज्ञानां भोक्ता च प्रभुरेव च ||
न तु मामभिजानन्ति तत्त्वेनातश्च्यवन्ति ते || 24||


ahaṁ hi sarva-yajñānāṁ bhoktā cha prabhureva cha
na tu mām abhijānanti tattvenātaś chyavanti te

Translation

I am the enjoyer and the only Lord of all sacrifices. But those who fail to realize My divine nature must be reborn.
