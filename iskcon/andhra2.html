Radha Gopinath Dasavatara Mandir -Rajahmundry

Sri Sri Radha Gopinath Mandir, also known as ISKCON Chowpatty, is a prominent temple located in Girgaon, South Mumbai. Established in 1988, it was envisioned by Srila Prabhupada in 1971 when he pointed to the site and expressed the desire for a temple there. The temple is renowned for its grand architecture, including Burmese teak wood and Italian marble interiors, and serves as a spiritual hub for thousands of devotees.

History

Sri Sri Radha Gopinath Mandir was established in 1988 in Mumbai, fulfilling Srila Prabhupada’s vision from 1971. The temple is known for its exquisite architecture featuring Burmese teak wood and Italian marble. Over the years, it has become a key spiritual center for devotees practicing bhakti yoga.
