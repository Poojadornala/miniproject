Radha Madhav Temple- Dakshin Marg

Sri Sri Radha Madhav Temple (ISKCON Chandigarh) is a serene spiritual hub established in 1978 by Srila Prabhupada, devoted to Lord Krishna and Radharani on a beautifully landscaped 3-acre campus. It features elegant marble architecture, daily aartis, bhajans, meditation programs, and a guest house for devotees .

Temple

History

Sri Sri Radha Madhav Temple was established in 1978 by Srila Prabhupada, the founder of ISKCON, as part of his mission to spread Krishna consciousness worldwide. Since then, it has grown into a major spiritual center in North India, attracting thousands of devotees and visitors annually.
