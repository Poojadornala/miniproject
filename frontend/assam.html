HARE KRISHNA TEMPLE

The Hare Krishna Temple in North Guwahati, Assam is a branch of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON), dedicated to the practice and promotion of the Hare Krishna movement, also known as the Gaudiya Vaishnavism tradition.

Temple

History

The temple in North Guwahati was established to provide a place for devotees to gather, worship, and practice their faith. It hosts regular bhajans, kirtans, arati ceremonies, and also provides prasad to visitors. Festivals such as Janmashtami and Holi are celebrated with great devotion.

Bhagavad Gita Slokam

Chapter 10, Verse 8

अहं सर्वस्य प्रभवो मत्त: सर्वं प्रवर्तते |
इति मत्वा भजन्ते मां बुधा भावसमन्विता: || 8 ||

ahaṁ sarvasya prabhavo mattaḥ sarvaṁ pravartate
iti matvā bhajante māṁ budhā bhāva-samanvitāḥ

Translation

I am the origin of all creation. Everything proceeds from Me. The wise who know this perfectly worship Me with great faith and devotion.
