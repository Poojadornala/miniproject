Baba Thakur

Baba Thakur is a temple dedicated to Krishna in Karoli village of Kosli tehsil of Rewari district in Haryana state of India. "Thakur" is another name of Krishna.

Temple

History

The temple is said to be 150 years old. In 1997, the villagers renovated the temple, the renovation took two years.

Bhagavad Gita Slokam

Chapter 9, Verse 4

मया ततमिदं सर्वं जगदव्यक्तमूर्तिना |
मत्स्थानि सर्वभूतानि न चाहं तेष्ववस्थित: || 4||

mayā tatam idaṁ sarvaṁ jagad avyakta-mūrtinā
mat-sthāni sarva-bhūtāni na chāhaṁ teṣhvavasthitaḥ

Translation

This entire cosmic manifestation is pervaded by Me in My unmanifest form. All living beings dwell in Me, but I do not dwell in them.
