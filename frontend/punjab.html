Shri Krishna Mandir, Ludhiana, Punjab

The Presiding Deity of this Temple is Lord Krishna and He is an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. This is one of the sacred and beautiful Temple that is located in the Model Town extension.

Temple

History

This Temple is built on a whopping 500 Square Yeard of land. This Temple is decorated using 520 idols of Gods and Goddesses. The 37 feet tall Hanuman Idol stands as an eyecatching feature of this Temple. This Temple management helps a Charitable Hospital to provide free Medical treatments to poor and needy. Every year Sri Krihna Janamashtami is celebrated in a grand manner ina open ground out side the Temple complex. Thousands of Deovtess visit this Temple on ausoicious days like Dushera and Shivrathri.

Festivals

The three festivals celebrated at Shri Krishna temple are Janmashtami, Dushera and Shivratri.

Bhagavad Gita Slokam

Chapter 9, Verse 27

यत्करोषि यदश्नासि यज्जुहोषि ददासि यत् |
यत्तपस्यसि कौन्तेय तत्कुरुष्व मदर्पणम् || 27||

yat karoṣhi yad aśhnāsi yaj juhoṣhi dadāsi yat
yat tapasyasi kaunteya tat kuruṣhva mad-arpaṇam

Translation

Whatever you do, whatever you eat, whatever you offer as oblation to the sacred fire, whatever you bestow as a gift, and whatever austerities you perform, O son of Kunti, do them as an offering to Me.
